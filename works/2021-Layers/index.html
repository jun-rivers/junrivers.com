_Layers_

Two non-parallel lines creates a plane. From the point of intersection, a line perpendicular to the plan extends from floor to ceiling, creating an open volume. Piercing 3 layers of metals, naturally aged. The AR layer empowers users to further compose this space with extra planes, suspended at random heights. The sprinkled lenses are but fractals of memory in this factory space once operated by a family of kind hearts and diligence. The audio tells a fraction of the story of these layered memories.
Points, lines, planes, volumes: the elements easily controlled.

《層》

兩條水平線的交叉點有一條垂直線頂天立地，營造了空間。 貫穿了三層的鏽鐵，散佈著已被遺忘的鏡片，記載了曾經在此做出成就的一家好人。 AR 層擴增實境，讓賞客也可以互動地增加縣浮的層塊，改變空間的體驗結構，猶如聽他人故事後的個人演繹。 聲道又錄述了童年的一層回憶。

點，線，面，體的基本可控元素，融合時間，回憶等非可控元素，至數碼互動這個被控制元素，層層堆疊，正是夢的建築。
